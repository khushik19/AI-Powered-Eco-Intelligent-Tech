## 🌊 The Problem
Global sustainability goals often feel out of reach because local impact is hard to measure and even harder to verify. College campuses generate massive energy, water, and e-waste footprints, but these actions go unmonitored. 

**The Gap:** Most existing solutions (like *Ma Petite Planète*) rely on the **Honor System** (unverified claims), lack institutional incentives (no link to NAAC/NIRF rankings), and fail to quantify real carbon savings.

## 🚀 The Solution: Clean Cosmos
Clean Cosmos is a dual-portal, AI-powered ecosystem that gamifies sustainability for both **Institutions** and **Individuals**. 

By using **Autonomous AI Vision Agents**, we move from "Trust" to "Proof." Every action—from a student cycling to a college installing solar panels—is verified, quantified into $CO_2$ metrics, and converted into **Stardust Points** and **Carbon Credit fractions**.

---

## 🛠 Key Features

### 1. 🤖 Nebula Chatbot
A high-fidelity researcher agent that provides factual, practical, and implementable sustainability advice. Unlike generic LLMs, Nebula uses a dedicated researcher pipeline to solve campus-specific resource optimization queries.

### 2. 👁️ AI Vision Agent (Autonomous Verification)
Powered by **OpenRouter (Gemini)**, this agent:
* **Autonomous Review:** Reviews uploaded images/videos and descriptions to verify authenticity.
* **Impact Quantification:** Calculates exact savings (e.g., "This action prevented 5kg of $CO_2$ emissions").
* **Automatic Rewarding:** Issues **Stardust Points** instantly upon approval.

### 3. 📊 Analytics & Inaction Highlighting
* **Sustainability Reports:** Comprehensive weekly/monthly/yearly reports aggregated from Firebase data.
* **Trend Analysis:** Graphical representation of sustainable practices via `fl_chart`.
* **Blind Spot Detection:** The AI identifies "Inaction Zones"—categories (Waste, Water, Energy) where the institution hasn't logged activities—prompting targeted improvements.

### 4. 🏆 Gamification & Rewards
* **Leaderboards:** Filterable by Institution, City, State, and Country.
* **Streaks & Challenges:** Weekly eco-challenges and streaks to ensure habit formation.
* **Carbon Credit Counter:** A live metric showing points equated to potential Carbon Credit assets.

## ⚡ The Competitive Edge (The "MPP Killer")
* **From "Trust" to "Proof":** Unlike other apps, we don't use the "Honor System." Our AI Vision creates **Certified Data** that governments and accrediting bodies can trust.
* **Institutional Hook:** Specifically designed for **NIRF/NAAC accreditation** and India’s **Green Credit Program (Mission LiFE)**.
* **Carbon Aggregator:** We democratize carbon markets by bundling micro-actions from thousands of students into sellable **Carbon Credits**.
